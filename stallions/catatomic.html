About Catatomic

Catatomic — 2006 Sorrel Stallion, 14.2 hands, AQHA registered. Standing at Silver Spurs Equine, Scottsdale AZ. Full breeding information, video, and booking available at silverspursequine.com/catatomic-1.

By High Brow Cat (High Brow Hickory x Smart Little Kitty/Smart Little Lena — LTE $110,784; retired 1995; NCHA-AQHA #1 All-Time Leading Sire 2003–2020; NRCHA All-Time Leading Sire; PE $91M+; sire of Metallic Cat, Smooth As A Cat, Dont Look Twice, Boon San Kitty, and thousands of performers across every western discipline; inducted into the AQHA Quarter Horse Hall of Fame; died October 2019 at age 31 — one of the most influential sires in the history of western performance horses) out of Miss Dual Cherlena (by Dual Peppy — adding proven cow horse and working bloodlines through the dam line, combining the elite cutting genetics of High Brow Cat on top with athletic cow-minded foundation mares below).

"Like Haboomamatada we tried to fit a square peg in a round hole and tried to make a reiner out of a natural cow horse." — Silver Spurs Equine on Catatomic's career

Catatomic's show career leaned into reining events — he placed in the finals at the High Roller Reining Classic, Reining By The Bay, and Tulsa Reining Classic in 2010, and won the AZ Sun Circuit Non-Pro Rookie Reserve Championship in 2013 — but by Silver Spurs' own account, his true calling was always on a cow. That natural affinity for cattle, inherited through both his High Brow Cat sire line and his Dual Peppy dam line, is exactly what has made him an all-around breeding stallion of note at Silver Spurs Equine. His offspring confirm it: earners span reining, roping, cutting, and barrel racing — a diversity of disciplines that speaks directly to his genetic versatility.

High Brow Cat's influence on the western performance horse industry is unmatched. In 2021, twelve of the top 25 cutting horse sires were sons or grandsons of High Brow Cat, and his combined progeny earnings through sons Metallic Cat and Smooth As A Cat alone exceed $94 million. Catatomic carries that same foundation — the elite cow sense, balanced athleticism, quick feet, and willingness to work that define the Cat line — in an accessible, proven package at Silver Spurs Equine. Breeders who want High Brow Cat genetics in their program without the premium price of frozen-only access to the reference sire find in Catatomic a live, proven option at an honest fee.

Catatomic is enrolled in the NRCHA Sire & Dam Program with eligible foal crops from 2020 through 2024. He is 5-Panel N/N clean across all AQHA genetic tests — a critical consideration for breeders crossing him on mares from Peptoboonsmal, High Brow Cat, or other HERDA-susceptible lines. Owned and standing at Silver Spurs Equine LLC, Scottsdale AZ. 2026 stud fee $2,000. Book online or request information at silverspursequine.com/catatomic-1.
