About Smart Shiners Spook

Smart Shiners Spook — 2012 Stallion, AQHA, 14.3 hands. Known at Silver Spurs as "Shines." Standing at Silver Spurs Equine, Scottsdale AZ. Full breeding details, video, and booking at silverspursequine.com/smart-shiners-spook.

By Smart Spook (Smart Chic Olena x Sugarplum Spook — LTE $405,000+; NRHA Hall of Fame; NRHA $7M+ Sire; NRHA Futurity Champion; NRHA Derby Champion; AQHA World Champion Junior Reining; AQHA Superior Reining; 2007 & 2009 FEI World Reining Masters Gold Medalist ridden by Rudi Kronsteiner; Smart Chic Olena, Smart Spook's sire, is an NRHA Hall of Fame inductee and $7M NRHA Sire; Smart Spook has produced 5 NRHA Futurity Champions, 1 NRHA Derby Champion, 2 NRHA Non-Pro Futurity Champions, NRHA Open and Reserve Open World Champions, and 15 offspring over $100,000 in NRHA LTE) out of Ebony Shines (Shining Spark x Ebony Whiz/Topsail Whiz — NRHA Hall of Fame inductee 2016; NRHA's first and only Million Dollar Dam; $961,000+ in offspring earnings; 16 NRHA earners; produce includes NRHA Open Futurity Co-Champion Shine N Spook, Shiners Chic $295,247 [second-highest Smart Chic Olena earner], Shine Chic Shine, Hang Ten And Shine, and Smart Shiners Spook). Smart Shiners Spook carries two Hall of Fame parents — Smart Spook and Ebony Shines — a distinction matched by almost no other breeding stallion in any Silver Spurs program.

"Knowing that Shines is such a great competitor, his mind writes checks that his body can't cash." — Michael Miola, Silver Spurs Equine

Trained and shown exclusively by Andrea Fappani, Smart Shiners Spook's show career is defined by exceptional talent cut short by recurring physical limitation — never by a lack of ability. In 2015, as a three-year-old, he entered the NRHA Open Futurity and placed 2nd in the first go-round and 3rd in the second go-round. A horse finishing that high across both preliminary rounds at the NRHA Futurity — the most prestigious three-year-old reining event in the world — is by definition among the very best of his generation. He was scratched from the finals that night due to injury, which cost him the title but did not diminish what his runs had proven. In 2016, Silver Spurs chose to give him a full recovery year rather than rush him back. He returned in 2017, finished 4th at the NRBC Open Derby, and then was retired to the breeding shed in 2018 — a decision made in the horse's best interest before any further competition risk. He also won the CRHA Open Futurity Championship and the Best of the West Open Futurity Championship during his career.

As a breeding stallion, Smart Shiners Spook's value proposition is anchored in pedigree depth that few horses at any fee level can match. His sire Smart Spook is an NRHA $7M Hall of Fame sire with five Futurity Champions in his offspring record. His dam Ebony Shines is NRHA's first and only Million Dollar Dam — a milestone so significant it made industry headlines — and an NRHA Hall of Fame inductee. Both parents were outstanding arena competitors who transmitted consistent talent, trainable minds, and powerful reining mechanics to every foal. Breeders crossing Smart Shiners Spook access the Smart Spook / Smart Chic Olena / Shining Spark / Topsail Whiz pedigree stack at Silver Spurs' most accessible price point.
