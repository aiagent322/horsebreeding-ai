About Chic Dreamin

Chic Dreamin — 2012 Sorrel Stallion, AQHA, 14.3 hands. Known throughout Silver Spurs as "Skeeter." Bred by Kathy and Casey Hinton. Owned by Silver Spurs Equine LLC, Scottsdale AZ. Full breeding information, video, and booking available at silverspursequine.com/chicdreamin.

By Magnum Chic Dream (Smart Chic Olena x Sailin Barbee/Topsail Cody — LTE $44,294; 2004 AQHA Senior Reining World Champion; AQHA Superior Reining; 100 AQHA points; NRHA Hall of Fame inductee 2016; NRHA $13M+ Sire — the fifth sire in NRHA history to cross $13 million in offspring earnings; nearly 900 money-earning progeny; 30 offspring each surpassing $100,000 in NRHA LTE; 2017 & 2018 RGP Top 5 Leading Sire of Reining money-earners; 2017 RGP #2 Leading Sire of Ranch Riding money-earners; ridden by Casey Hinton throughout his show career) out of Skeets Red Dunit (2007 — by Skeets Peppy/Peppy San Badger x Brennas Red Dunit/Hollywood Dun It; A Leading Producer; dam of 4 money-earners totaling $330,776; dam of Chic Dreamin and full sibling Magnum Chic Dunit [$44,184 — NRHA Open Futurity finalist]).

"It never gets old to win anywhere, especially not here. It's a special place and holds a special place in my heart because this is where I marked a 236, which is still today the highest in NRHA history, so it's a great place where you can really showcase your horse." — Andrea Fappani, after the 2017 NRBC Championship on Chic Dreamin

Andrea Fappani — one of the sport's all-time leading money earners — described the NRBC as his favorite venue, the place where he once marked a 236 that remains the highest score in NRHA history. Winning the 2017 Level 4 Open Classic Championship there with Chic Dreamin, scoring 229 in the final run of the night and collecting the $75,000 paycheck, was Fappani's fourth NRBC Open Championship title. He returned the following year and won the 2018 Reining By The Bay Open Derby Championship with a 232 — an even higher score that confirmed Skeeter's peak performance was not a one-event peak. Between those two marquee wins he also won the 2017 AQHA World Champion Junior Reining title and the 2016 NRBC Intermediate Open Derby Championship — a consistent trail of top-level results across three separate seasons.

As a breeding stallion, Chic Dreamin carries the full depth of the Magnum Chic Dream pedigree — Smart Chic Olena athleticism and trainability on top, Topsail Cody reining foundation through Sailin Barbee, and Peppy San Badger cow sense and competitive drive through Skeets Peppy in the dam. Magnum Chic Dream's sire Smart Chic Olena is by NCHA Triple Crown winner Smart Little Lena — one of the sport's most foundational sire lines — giving Chic Dreamin a four-generation pedigree depth rarely matched at his fee. Breeders looking for Magnum Chic Dream lineage at an accessible price point find in Chic Dreamin a World Champion and NRBC Champion son of a $13M Hall of Fame sire, standing at Silver Spurs Equine.
