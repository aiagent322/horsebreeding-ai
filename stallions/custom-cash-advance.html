About Custom Cash Advance

Custom Cash Advance — 2006 Sorrel Stallion, AQHA, 15 hands. Known throughout the Silver Spurs program as "Cash." Standing at Silver Spurs Equine, Scottsdale AZ. Full breeding information, video, and booking available at silverspursequine.com/custom-cash-advance-1.

By Custom Crome (Crome Plated Jac x Another Greyhound/Greyhound Step — LTE $116,577; 1993 NRHA Open Futurity Champion; NRHA $4M Equi-Stat Elite Sire; NRHA Top 10 All-Time Leading Sire; #6 NRHA All-Time Leading Sire; 408 money-earning foals; shown by Mike Flarida for the majority of his career; known for passing reining mechanics and exceptional kind demeanor to offspring in every generation) out of Cash In Roan (Topsail Cody x — Topsail Cody: NRHA Million Dollar Sire and Hall of Fame inductee, sire of Topsail Whiz who became the NRHA $12 Million Sire; Cash In Roan is a confirmed Leading NRHA Producer per Silver Spurs Equine; the Topsail Cody dam line connects Custom Cash Advance to the Topsail family at the most foundational level possible — through Topsail Cody himself rather than through any of his more widely distributed sons). Custom Cash Advance stands as the #2 all-time earner among Custom Crome's offspring, behind only Custom Pistol ($254,858).

Trained and shown throughout his career by Andrea Fappani — one of the most decorated riders in NRHA history and a consistent presence at the top of Silver Spurs' competition program — Custom Cash Advance built a career defined by sustained elite-level consistency. His 2009 Tulsa Reining Open Futurity Championship launched a record that would span six consecutive years of major event competition. Placing 3rd at the NRHA Open Derby in both 2011 and 2012 — back-to-back top-three finishes at one of the sport's premier events — is an accomplishment matched by only a handful of horses in NRHA history. The NRHA Open Derby draws the world's best open reining professionals and the most talented horses in the sport; a single top-three finish is a career landmark. Doing it in consecutive years confirms that Custom Cash Advance's talent was consistent rather than a single-season peak.

The 2013 NRBC 7UP Open Challenge Championship capped a multi-season career that also included top-10 finishes at the AQHA World Show, Cactus Reining Classic, Cowboy Capital Classic, High Roller Classic, and NRBC Open Derby across multiple years — confirming cross-event versatility at the highest levels. His Custom Crome sire line is the most commercially distinct pedigree on the Silver Spurs roster. While the majority of Silver Spurs stallions represent Colonels Smoking Gun, Topsail Whiz, and Shining Spark sire lines, Custom Cash Advance brings Custom Crome genetics — a genuine pedigree outcross for any mare already carrying heavy Gunner, Whiz, or Spark blood. Breeders whose mares descend from Wimpys Little Step, Shining Spark, or Colonels Smoking Gun find in Custom Cash Advance a cross that combines elite NRHA sire production from a complementary line without concentration risk. Offspring confirmed competing in AQHA ranch riding as well as NRHA reining confirm the cross-discipline versatility the Custom Crome x Topsail Cody combination consistently produces. Full information and booking at silverspursequine.com/custom-cash-advance-1.

5-Panel N/N. Cooled and frozen semen available. Enrolled in NRHA Sire & Dam, NRBC, and Silver Spurs $1 Million Challenge. 2026 stud fee $3,000. Standing Silver Spurs Equine LLC, Scottsdale AZ. Contact Silver Spurs at silverspursequine.com/custom-cash-advance-1.
