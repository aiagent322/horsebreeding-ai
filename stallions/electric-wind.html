About Electric Wind

Electric Wind — 2013 Sorrel Stallion, AQHA, 14.3 hands. Known as "Hobbs" to his connections. Standing at Silver Spurs Equine, Scottsdale AZ. Full breeding information, video, and booking available at silverspursequine.com/electricwind. Bred by Oldenburg Farm LLC of Aubrey, Texas. Owned by Silver Spurs Equine LLC.

By Jacs Electric Spark (Shining Spark x Miss Hello Hollywood/Hollywood Jac 86 — LTE $85,907; NRHA Hall of Fame; NRHA $10.3M+ offspring earnings; Equi-Stat All-Time Leading Reined Cow Horse Sire; Top 10 All-Time Leading Reining Sire; offspring earnings include producers with get earnings exceeding $16M; Shining Spark, Jacs Electric Spark's sire, is the NRHA Hall of Fame inductee and foundational reining sire whose sons and daughters dominate the Silver Spurs roster; Jacs Electric Spark himself stands at Silver Spurs Equine and is a proven all-around performance sire across reining, reined cow horse, and ranch riding) out of Wind Her Up Chic (Smart Chic Olena x — Smart Chic Olena: NRHA Hall of Fame; NRHA $7M+ Sire; $11M+ PE across reining, cutting, cow horse, barrel racing, and team roping; dam of Voodoo Chic, mother of Shiners Voodoo Dr in this database; the Smart Chic Olena connection through Wind Her Up Chic adds elite trainability, cow sense, and the cross-discipline versatility that makes Electric Wind an all-around breeding prospect).

"In the horse business, we make all these plans, but you can't predict if they are going to work or not. For this plan to have worked out like it did — right before he's going to start his breeding career and everything — was just perfect." — Trevor Dare, after the 2019 AQHA World Show

Electric Wind's 2019 campaign was a masterclass in strategic show management. Silver Spurs and trainer Trevor Dare made the deliberate decision to show the stallion on a limited basis throughout the year, conserving him for the AQHA World Championship Show in Oklahoma City. Heading into the World Show, Hobbs had just $13,326 in lifetime earnings and had not yet won a major title. What followed validated the plan completely: Electric Wind posted a score of 225 in the Senior Reining Level 3 final to claim the World Championship title — the first and defining title of his career, captured precisely when his connections needed it most, right before his move to the breeding shed. Dare described the stallion as feeling "fresh, very healthy and strong" through the final, noting that the limited show schedule had left him sharper than competitors who had been shown more heavily throughout the season. The night before the finals, Dare said Hobbs gave him "the best schooling ride I've ever had on him."

Dare described Electric Wind's best qualities as "his talent and ability to stop and his great mind" — and for a six-year-old stallion, noted he was "very sweet to be around and to take to the arena." Those are the traits that transfer generation to generation: the Jacs Electric Spark quick feet and natural athletic stop paired with the Smart Chic Olena intelligence and calm, workmanlike temperament. Silver Spurs describes Electric Wind as "one of Jacs Electric Sparks best" and notes the current $1,000 fee is specifically positioned to build a book while the stallion proves his offspring. Breeders accessing Electric Wind now are betting on a World Champion with elite sire-line genetics — Jacs Electric Spark's Hall of Fame status and All-Time Leading Reined Cow Horse Sire title confirm the production depth behind this cross.
