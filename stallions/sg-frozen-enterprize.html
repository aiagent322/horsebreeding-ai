About SG Frozen Enterprize

SG Frozen Enterprize — 2009 Red Dun Stallion, AQHA #5266682, 15 hands. Known throughout the industry as "Iceman." Standing at Silver Spurs Equine, Purcell OK. Full breeding information, video, and booking available at silverspursequine.com/sg-frozen-enterprize. Owned by Mike and Michelle Miola.

By Frozen Sailor (LTE $9,788; 4th German Americana Reining Open; Italian Reining Open Derby Champion; Italy NRHA Portogruaro Open Champion; Italian Limited Open Reining Derby Champion; Italy NRHA Portogruaro Limited Open Co-Reserve Champion; sire of offspring with earnings in excess of $838,400, including producers with get earnings exceeding $178,600) out of RS Rose SJ (by SJ Enterprise; 1999 Sorrel mare, AQHA #3832307 — an Italian-bred dam line that combined with Frozen Sailor's European reining bloodlines to produce one of the most internationally competitive American reining stallions of his generation).

Iceman's show career is among the most geographically wide-ranging in the Silver Spurs program. He opened his record in Italy in 2012, winning the Italian RHA Futurity Open, Intermediate Open, and Limited Open championships — three titles at a single major event — before returning to the United States to continue building one of the most decorated résumés in his division. By 2013 he was placing in the NRHA Open Derby and winning the High Roller Reining 4-Year-Old championship with trainer Andrea Fappani. In 2014 he captured the AQHA World Championship Junior Reining title, and in 2015 delivered his marquee performance: the Level 4 Open Championship at the National Reining Breeders Classic, one of the three premier annual NRHA major events. He also captured the 2015 Cactus Classic Open Derby Championship. Over his career, Iceman accumulated 8 championship titles across the United States and Europe — a breadth of competition rarely matched in reining.

In the breeding shed, SG Frozen Enterprize has proven the depth of his genetics at every level of the sport. His first foal crop delivered an immediate statement: Icecube won the Level 4 NRHA Non-Pro Futurity Championship and went on to earn $176,759 including the Run For A Million Non-Pro Co-Championship. Savage Love has earned $181,852 including a top-10 finish at the NRHA Level 4 Open Futurity. Spooks Gotta Ice won the 100X Reining Classic 4YO Stakes L4 Open Championship. Froze won the NRHA Futurity Million Dollar Challenge L3 Non-Pro Championship. With $1,969,770+ in confirmed offspring earnings and NRHA Million Dollar Sire status, Iceman's sire career has fully validated his exceptional show record. Breeders selecting SG Frozen Enterprize gain access to proven performance genetics from an internationally competitive stallion with a first foal crop that produced an NRHA Futurity Champion. Full information and booking at silverspursequine.com/sg-frozen-enterprize.
