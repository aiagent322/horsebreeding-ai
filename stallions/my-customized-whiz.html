About My Customized Whiz

My Customized Whiz — 2013 Liver Chestnut Stallion, AQHA registered, 14.3 hands. Known at Silver Spurs Equine as "Wally." Standing at Silver Spurs Equine, Scottsdale AZ. Full breeding details, photos, and booking available at silverspursequine.com/mycustomizedwhiz.

By Walla Walla Whiz (Topsail Whiz x Shiney Walla Wanda/Shining Spark — LTE $187,820; NRHA $5.5M+ Sire; NRHA Futurity Champion Sire; 2007 NRHA Open Derby Champion; 2007 NRBC Open Derby 3rd; 2008 Wimpys Little Step Open Reining Derby Reserve Champion; 6-panel N/N; standing at Oswood Stallion Station, Weatherford TX; owned by Arcese family) out of Custom Mahogany, carrying Colonels Smoking Gun, Topsail Whiz, and Smart Chic Olena bloodlines through the dam line — a pedigree composition that layers three of the most proven reining sire lines of the modern era across a single cross. Walla Walla Whiz's own sire, Topsail Whiz, is the NRHA's first and only $12 Million Dollar Sire. His dam Shiney Walla Wanda is a daughter of Shining Spark, himself a $4M+ NRHA sire. The three-generation pedigree behind My Customized Whiz represents an unbroken line of elite reining producers.

My Customized Whiz did not show due to injury, and has no confirmed competition earnings — Silver Spurs Equine and Stallion Central both confirm this honestly. What he offers is pure pedigree: access to Walla Walla Whiz genetics at the most accessible price in the Silver Spurs lineup. Walla Walla Whiz himself also experienced injury early in his career, limiting his futurity experience, yet went on to become one of the most consistent NRHA sire-line producers of his generation — a fact that speaks directly to the depth of this bloodline even when show records are limited. Trainer Shawn Flarida, who piloted Walla Walla Whiz, described the stallion as "so good-minded" — a characteristic trait well documented across the Walla Walla Whiz offspring. That same calm, tractable intelligence is the hallmark of the Whiz line at every level.

Walla Walla Whiz's top offspring include Americasnexttopgun ($425,000+ with Casey Deary), Shesouttayourleague (2015 NRHA Open Futurity Champion and NRHA Open Derby Champion, $260,000+ with Casey Deary), Americas Supermodel ($230,000+), Isnt She Perfect (NRHA Open Futurity Reserve Champion, $170,000+), and Made In Walla (2015 NRHA European Open Futurity Champion, $130,000+). The sire line is internationally proven, with Walla Walla Whiz offspring earning in the United States and across Europe. My Customized Whiz gives breeders a direct link to this producing line at the entry level of the Silver Spurs program.

Silver Spurs Equine is currently running a Million Dollar Challenge Discount on My Customized Whiz, reducing his breeding fee for the entire 2026 season. A special combination offer is also available: purchase a breeding to My Customized Whiz and receive a second breeding to any Silver Spurs junior stallion for only $200 — an exceptional value for breeders looking to maximize production from multiple Whiz-line crosses.
