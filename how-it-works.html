How the Matchmaker Works

Enter your mare's discipline, bloodlines, and desired traits. Our scoring engine evaluates all 26 Silver Spurs stallions and returns the best match from each price tier — in under 2 seconds.

THE PROCESS
01
Enter Your Mare's Profile
Select your mare's primary discipline — reining, reined cow horse, cutting, or ranch. Add her sire and key bloodlines, then select the traits you want to improve or complement in her foal.
02
We Score Every Stallion
The matchmaker scores all 26 stallions against your mare using a deterministic 100-point algorithm. Every stallion gets a score based on discipline alignment, bloodline compatibility, and trait overlap.
03
AI Writes Your Explanation
Claude AI reads your mare's profile and the top-scoring stallions, then writes a one-sentence explanation specific to your mare — not a generic description. It tells you exactly why this cross works.
04
You Get 3 Tiered Results
Results are grouped by tier — Premium, Best Value, and Budget — so you can make a decision based on both match quality and budget. Each result shows the score, fee, offspring stats, and confidence level.

Category Points How It's Calculated
Discipline Match 40 pts Exact match only. If the stallion's listed disciplines include your mare's discipline, 40 points are awarded. No partial credit — discipline alignment is the most important factor.
Bloodline Compatibility 30 pts We check each stallion's bloodlines against your mare's bloodlines using a proven compatibility map of reining and cow horse sire lines. Complementary crosses earn points. Repeated bloodlines (line breeding risk) lose points. No bloodlines entered = neutral 15 points.
Trait Match 30 pts We compare the traits you selected for your mare against each stallion's known traits. The more overlap, the higher the score. Scored proportionally — 3 of 3 traits matched = full 30 points.
Tier Bonus +10 pts The Greats tier stallions receive a bonus for proven elite production records. Main Roster stallions receive +5. Junior stallions receive no bonus.

Frequently Asked Questions
Do I need to enter bloodlines to get a result?
No. Bloodlines are optional. If left blank, the bloodline category defaults to a neutral 15 points for all stallions. Entering bloodlines improves accuracy by rewarding complementary crosses and flagging repeated lines.
What if my mare's discipline isn't listed?
Select the closest discipline. Reined cow horse is the best choice for horses that compete in both reining and cow work. Ranch covers versatility, trail, and non-competitive horses.
Are the fees current?
All fees reflect Silver Spurs Equine's published 2026 breeding season pricing, including the mid-season reductions on HF Mobster, Pale Face Dunnit, and Platinum Vintage, and the free breedings on Chic Dreamin and Electric Wind.
How do I order a breeding contract?
Click any stallion result to go to his profile page, then use the "Order Breeding Contract" form. You can also visit the Silver Spurs Equine website directly via the "More Info" button on each profile.
Is this tool affiliated with Silver Spurs Equine?
HorseBreeding.ai is an independent tool built using publicly available Silver Spurs Equine stallion data. All breeding inquiries and contracts are handled directly by Silver Spurs Equine in Scottsdale, AZ and Purcell, OK.
What is ICSI breeding?
Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection (ICSI) is an advanced reproductive technique that allows breeding to retired or deceased stallions using frozen semen. Several Silver Spurs stallions — including Wimpys Little Step, A Sparkling Vintage, and Big Chex To Cash — are available via ICSI only. Silver Spurs' 2026 program lets you pay the breeding fee only after a live foal is on the ground.
